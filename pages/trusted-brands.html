Brands & Labs We Trust

Independent Recommendations — No Referral Fees, No Sponsorships

We don't take referral fees, affiliate commissions, or sponsorship money. These are brands, labs, and organizations we've worked with or evaluated over 15+ years in the field. We recommend them because they do good work — nothing more.

Mold Testing & Analysis Labs

These labs process air samples, surface samples, and ERMI/HERTSMI-2 dust tests. They provide species identification, spore counts, and analytical reports used by inspectors and homeowners.

EMLab P&K

One of the largest environmental testing labs in the US. AIHA-accredited. Processes air-o-cell, spore trap, and culturable samples. Fast turnaround.

Mycometrics

Specializes in ERMI and HERTSMI-2 testing via MSQPCR. Developed with the EPA. The gold standard for DNA-based mold analysis in dust samples.

EMSL Analytical

Full-service environmental lab. Offers mold, asbestos, lead, bacteria, and VOC analysis. AIHA-accredited with labs nationwide.

Air Quality Monitors & Sensors

Consumer and professional-grade monitors for tracking humidity, particulate matter, VOCs, CO2, and other indoor air quality metrics.

AirThings

Consumer-grade monitors for radon, humidity, temperature, VOCs, CO2, and particulate matter. App-based dashboard with historical tracking.

Temtop

Affordable particulate matter and air quality monitors. Good entry-level option for homeowners wanting to track PM2.5 and HCHO levels.

PurpleAir

Real-time PM2.5 sensor with a public data network. Useful for outdoor and indoor particulate monitoring. Community-driven air quality map.

Professional Organizations & Standards

Industry organizations that set standards for mold inspection, remediation, and indoor air quality work. When vetting a contractor or inspector, look for certifications from these bodies.

IICRC

Institute of Inspection, Cleaning and Restoration Certification. Publishes the S520 Standard for mold remediation — the industry benchmark for proper mold removal.

ACAC

American Council for Accredited Certification. Provides certifications for mold inspectors (CMI), remediators (CMR), and indoor air quality professionals.

AIHA

American Industrial Hygiene Association. Accredits environmental testing labs (EMLAP program). Look for AIHA accreditation when choosing a lab.

Dehumidifiers & Moisture Control

Moisture control is the foundation of mold prevention. These brands make reliable dehumidifiers and moisture management equipment.

AprilAire

Whole-home dehumidifiers and ventilation systems. Designed to integrate with existing HVAC systems for centralized humidity control.

Santa Fe

High-capacity commercial and residential dehumidifiers. Industry standard for crawlspace and basement moisture control.

hOmeLabs

Affordable portable dehumidifiers for individual rooms. Good entry-level option for managing humidity in smaller spaces.
